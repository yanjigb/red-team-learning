A TLD is the most righthand part of a domain name
There are two types of TLD, gTLD (Generic Top Level) and ccTLD (Country Code Top Level Domain)

**A gTLD** was meant to tell the user the domain name's purpose; for example,

a .com would be for commercial purposes
.org for an organisation
a ccTLD was used for geographical purposes
an influx of new gTLDs ranging from .online , .club , .website , .biz
